Subject Notes

Mathematics
Algebra

Study of mathematical symbols and the rules for manipulating these symbols. It includes concepts such as variables, equations, and polynomials.

Geometry

Branch of mathematics concerned with properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, and solids.

Trigonometry

Study of relationships between the angles and sides of triangles.

Calculus

Mathematical study of continuous change, dealing with derivatives and integrals.

Science
Physics

Study of matter, energy, and their interactions. Topics include mechanics, electromagnetism, and thermodynamics.

Chemistry

Science of substances, their properties, reactions, and the use of chemical processes to form new substances.

Biology

Study of living organisms, including their structure, function, growth, and evolution.

English
Grammar

Study of syntax, sentence structure, and word usage to ensure effective communication.

Literature

Analysis of written works, including poetry, prose, and drama, to understand deeper meanings and cultural significance.

Writing Skills

Development of essays, reports, and creative writing techniques to enhance clarity and expression.

Computer Science
Programming

Introduction to coding languages such as Python, Java, and C++. Focuses on algorithms and problem-solving techniques.

Data Structures

Study of organizing and managing data efficiently using arrays, linked lists, stacks, and trees.

Networking

Understanding of internet protocols, cybersecurity, and how data is transmitted across networks.
